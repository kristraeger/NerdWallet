About Me

I am a Front End Engineer who is passionate about user experience and is excited about creating web pages for NerdWallet that will impact million of users! I love Javascript and openly admit it. Based in San Francisco.

My Story

In the past 8 years, i have collaborated with engineers, product owners, and designers to bring products to market that enliven everyday life. I’ve been an influential contributor in my former career as a business exec & product manager in a variety of industries - automotive, delivery services, fashion e-commerce, tech and most recently as part of the founding team of a Y-combinator startup building a peer-to-peer fashion rental marketplace. In order to enrich my contribution to the product development process, i decided to learn how to code, and it’s been the best decision in my life! Building fast and seamless user experiences absolutely excites me! My German heritage has also ingrained me from a very young age to deliver excellent work, efficiently and on time - all valuable strengths to have as an engineer.

My Motivation

One of my mentors recently said “I’ve never met someone who’s more addicted to coding than Kristina” --- which makes me a great asset to your nerd culture! Being foreign, i especially understand the need in creating a transparent and highly-informative financial environment in the US. I would love to join NerdWallet’s front end engineering team, extend and improve the existing framework and explore new technologies. I am a very visual person (yes, pixel perfect coding is a thing for me!) so building out a style guide sounds like a fantastic challenge to work on!
